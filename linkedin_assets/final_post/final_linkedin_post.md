At the Hack4Future national-level hackathon, our team set out to solve a problem that affects millions: the barrier to accessible digital reading.

## The Problem
Reading isn't just about looking at words; it's about focus and accessibility. For users with dyslexia, ADHD, or visual strain, standard digital interfaces are often obstacles. Reading fatigue is real, and comprehension suffers when tools aren't built for inclusivity.

## The Solution: SquintScale
We built **SquintScale**, an accessibility-first reading assistant that adapts to the reader, not the other way around. 

**Key Technical Highlights:**
- 📸 **Camera OCR**: Real-time physical text extraction.
- 🌐 **URL Reader**: Distraction-free article parsing.
- 🧠 **Focus Modes**: Specialized ADHD and Dyslexia-friendly overlays.
- 📊 **Session Analytics**: Tracking reading health and focus patterns.
- 🐳 **Infrastructure**: Fully Dockerized FastAPI + Supabase backend.
